Reset BMC to Default

When you reset the BMC, your web browser loses contact with the BMC for several minutes and all the changes wil be erased. When the BMC is back online, you must log in again. If the Log In button is not available when the BMC is brought back online, close your web browser. Then, reopen the web browser and enter your BMC IP address.
